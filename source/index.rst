About Synapse
=============

Synapse is a Science Gateway for the
`CNAP Center <http://www.k-state.edu/cnap/>`_ at Kansas State University.  As a
Science Gateway, Synapse aims to provide a user-friendly interface to research
computing resources for the psychological sciences including:

* High performance computing (`Beocat <https://beocat.ksu.edu/>`_)
* Data Storage, Transfer, and Sharing
* Domain-Specific Visualization
* Collaborative Interfaces
* Dataset Hosting and Publication

Funding
=======

Synapse and The CNAP Center of Biomedical Research Excellence (COBRE) is funded
through a $10.6M grant from the National Institute of General Medical Sciences
(P20 GM113109) in 2017.
